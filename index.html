Our Story

Hi There!

We’re Hamwise Studios and we are a passionate two-person team based in Melbourne, Australia. We pour our hearts into creating games that offer players an escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Our belief is simple - gaming should be accessible and inclusive to all, irrespective of age or skill level. With this in mind, we work tirelessly to create games that both challenge and entertain, catering to a diverse audience while maintaining a friendly and inviting aesthetic.

So come and join us on this exciting journey. Whether you’re trying to aim some potatoes into a rotating circle in Rotatoes, or navigating a family of dolphins past obstacles in Dolphams, Hamwise Studios has something for everyone.
