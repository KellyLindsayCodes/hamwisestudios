Privacy Policy

About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Hamwise Studios collects and uses your personal information when you use the game.

Why is information collected?

Information is collected to assist us in analysing how our service is used to help to provide a better product and to provide advertising that suits the user. These are collected through the use of Unity Analytics and ironSource. For more information, see the following privacy policies: Unity, ironSource.

We only collect data with the intent of achieving these goals. We may employ employ third party service providers to provide a service on our behalf or to assist in the analysis of how our service is used.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy. Any updates will be posted on this page.
